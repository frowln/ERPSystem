## 2. Business Sense Evaluation

### Margins — Are They Healthy?

| Item | Margin% | Assessment |
|------|---------|------------|
| Кабель ВВГнг | 26.72% | GREEN — healthy for materials (15-40% normal) |
| Автомат АВВ | 16.67% | GREEN — acceptable for equipment |
| Монтаж кабельных линий | 100.00% | YELLOW — suspicious, likely missing subcontractor cost |
| Воздуховод оцинк. | 22.45% | GREEN — healthy |
| Монтаж воздуховодов | 100.00% | YELLOW — same issue: works have no costPrice |

**Overall margin 52.78%** — HIGH. This is because work items (3 and 5) show 100% margin
since there's no subcontractor cost in costPrice. In real projects, the прораб would see
these as free money, but the бухгалтер knows the labor cost is somewhere.

**Business verdict**: The system correctly shows margin, but WORK items need a mechanism
to capture labor/subcontractor costs (either from КЛ for subcontractors, or from internal
labor rates). Without this, the margin is inflated and misleading for the директор.

### Document Chain — Is It Complete?

```
Specification ✓ → КЛ ✓ → FM ✓ ← ЛСР ✓ → КП ✓ → Contract ?
```

The chain Spec→КЛ→FM←ЛСР→КП is fully implemented and tested. The missing link is
КП→Contract (contract generation from approved КП). This is a GAP but not a blocker
for pre-construction operations.

### НДС Compliance

- НДС is exactly 20% of customerTotal — **CORRECT** per Russian tax law (НК РФ, ст. 164)
- НДС is displayed separately (violet color) — matches accounting requirements
- No НДС on costPrice side — this is correct for net pricing

### Regulatory Compliance

- **КЛ minimum 3 vendors**: tested and enforced — matches 223-ФЗ procurement requirements
- **ГЭСН-based estimates**: estimatePrice comes from ЛСР (ГЭСН norms) — compliant
- **Overhead/profit rates**: 12%/8% — within MDS 81-33.2004 / MDS 81-25.2001 ranges

## 4. UX Observations by Persona

### Прораб (Site Foreman)
- **Pain**: The FM page has too many columns and numbers. A прораб needs "what materials to order" and "when", not margin percentages.
- **Suggestion**: Add a simplified "Снабжение" view that shows only: item name, quantity needed, ordered (Y/N), delivery date.
- **Positive**: КЛ auto-select saves time — no manual vendor comparison needed.

### Бухгалтер (Accountant)
- **Positive**: НДС is exactly 20%, calculated automatically — no manual errors.
- **Positive**: KPI strip shows all key totals at a glance.
- **Pain**: Cannot export FM to Excel with formulas. Needs "Выгрузить в Excel" button.
- **Pain**: No way to see accumulated НДС across multiple projects for quarterly reporting.
- **Critical**: Work items showing 100% margin is misleading. Бухгалтер would flag this immediately.

### Директор (CEO)
- **Positive**: Margin% of 52.78% is prominently displayed — shows profitability instantly.
- **Pain**: Can only see one project at a time. Needs portfolio-level margin dashboard.
- **Suggestion**: Add comparison view: "How does this project's margin compare to company average?"
- **Positive**: Color-coded margins (green ≥15%) make it easy to spot problem items.

### Инженер-сметчик (Cost Engineer)
- **Positive**: ЛСР→FM price matching works correctly.
- **Positive**: estimatePrice vs customerPrice comparison shows where we deviate from norms.
- **Pain**: No visual indicator for "customerPrice < estimatePrice" (we're giving a discount).
- **Suggestion**: Add amber warning when tradingCoefficient < 1.0 (selling below estimate).

### Снабженец (Procurement Manager)
- **Positive**: КЛ with 3+ vendor requirement enforces procurement best practices.
- **Positive**: Auto-ranking with weighted scoring (price + delivery + warranty + payment terms) is industry-leading.
- **Pain**: Cannot attach vendor commercial proposals (PDF/scan) to КЛ entries.
- **Suggestion**: Add "Прикрепить файл" to each КЛ entry for audit trail.

## 5. Prioritized Improvement List

### P0 — Must Fix (Blockers)

1. **[CRITICAL] Work items need costPrice mechanism**
   - Works show 100% margin because costPrice=0
   - Solution: Allow КЛ entries for subcontractor works, OR internal labor rate calculator
   - Impact: Директор sees inflated margins, makes wrong decisions
   - Effort: 2-3 days

2. **[CRITICAL] КП→Contract generation**
   - The chain stops at КП. No automatic contract creation from approved КП.
   - Solution: "Сформировать договор" button on approved КП → populate contract template
   - Impact: Manual contract creation is error-prone and slow
   - Effort: 3-5 days

### P1 — High Priority

3. **[MAJOR] FM Excel export with formulas**
   - Бухгалтер needs to export FM to Excel for management reporting
   - Include: all columns, section subtotals, KPI row, НДС column
   - Effort: 1-2 days

4. **[MAJOR] Trading coefficient display in КП**
   - Show tradingCoefficient = customerPrice / estimatePrice for each item
   - Color: green (1.0-1.3), amber (0.7-1.0), red (<0.7 or >2.0)
   - Effort: 0.5 day

5. **[MAJOR] КЛ vendor file attachments**
   - Attach КП/прайс-лист PDF from vendor to each КЛ entry
   - Required for procurement audit trail (ISO 9001)
   - Effort: 1-2 days

### P2 — Medium Priority

6. **[UX] Simplified procurement view for прораб**
   - Separate page/tab: "Снабжение" with item, qty, ordered, delivery date
   - Hides financial details (margins, prices)
   - Effort: 1 day

7. **[UX] Portfolio-level margin dashboard**
   - Aggregate margin% across all active projects
   - Table: project name, customerTotal, costTotal, margin%, status
   - Effort: 1-2 days

8. **[MINOR] customerPrice < estimatePrice warning**
   - Visual indicator when selling below estimate (discount mode)
   - Not an error, but a conscious decision that should be tracked
   - Effort: 0.5 day

### P3 — Nice to Have

9. **[UX] КЛ scoring weights configurable**
   - Currently hardcoded: price 50%, delivery 25%, warranty 15%, payment 10%
   - Allow project-level or company-level customization
   - Effort: 1 day

10. **[MISSING] ЛСР version comparison**
    - When re-importing ЛСР, show diff: "Price changed from X to Y on 15 items"
    - Critical for re-estimation scenarios (корректировочная смета)
    - Effort: 2-3 days

11. **[MISSING] КП versioning with diff view**
    - Track changes between КП versions (v1 → v2)
    - Show: which items changed, by how much, who approved
    - Effort: 2-3 days

12. **[MISSING] Integration with СБИС/Контур for ЭДО**
    - Send КС-2/КС-3 generated from FM via electronic document exchange
    - Required for large contractors (mandatory since 2024)
    - Effort: 2-4 weeks (API integration)

## Summary

The financial chain (Spec→КЛ→FM←ЛСР→КП) is the **unique competitive advantage** of PRIVOD.
No Russian competitor (1С, Б24, PlanRadar, Мегаплан) offers this integrated flow.
Internationally, only Procore comes close, but lacks Russian regulatory compliance.

**Test coverage**: 165 assertions across 26 steps verify every number in the chain.

**Critical gaps**: Work item costing (P0) and КП→Contract link (P0) are the two blockers
that prevent the system from being production-complete for the full pre-construction cycle.

**Business verdict**: The system is strong for material procurement (Spec→КЛ→FM with real
vendor pricing). It needs work for services/subcontractor costing. The НДС and margin
calculations are mathematically correct and regulatory-compliant.
